About

I am a PhD student in Electrical & Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin, advised by Prof. Ufuk Topcu. My research focuses on algorithms for balancing competing objectives in multi-agent and multi-task settings, with applications to robotics and urban air mobility.

I design comparison-aware optimization methods that integrate human or preference feedback and remain robust to non-affine transformations of task losses. Recently I’ve worked on a unified RL framework for noise, energy, and safety management in UAM traffic.
